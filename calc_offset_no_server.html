I have chosen to create a personal carbon footprint calculator for bus transportation because I take the bus everywhere on a daily basis. I got the lbCo2 per passenger mile coefficient from this website. I copied over the carbon advanced number 2 code into my webpage. I had to convert the lbCO2 per passenger mile to lbCO2 per passenger kilometers. I believe the ability to calculate it in different systems of measures, such as kilometres and miles, makes the code much more robust and flexible to much more users. However, this code could be brought to another level. It could calculate your total carbon footprint for your entire day by merging the emissions from the multiple modes of transportation, this could even be an iDevice app (it might've existed already).
